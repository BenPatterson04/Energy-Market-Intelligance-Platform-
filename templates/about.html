What is GridWise?

GridWise is an energy market intelligence platform bringing together wholesale market data, i-SEM (Ireland/Northern Ireland electricity market) data, UK grid status, domestic energy reference prices, weather, and curated energy news into one dashboard - with an AI-generated daily briefing written for both general readers and energy professionals.

Data Sources

  • Wholesale markets (Brent, gas, indices, FX) — Yahoo Finance
  • UK grid carbon intensity & generation mix — National Grid ESO Carbon Intensity API
  • i-SEM demand & wind generation — ENTSO-E Transparency Platform
  • i-SEM carbon intensity & wind share — Electricity Maps
  • UK & global energy news — NewsAPI
  • Weather — OpenWeather
  • AI market commentary — Claude (Anthropic)

Domestic tariffs are currently reference/example figures rather than a live per-supplier feed - clearly labelled as such on the Domestic tab.
